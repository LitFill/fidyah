# Initial Concept

website static untuk perhitungan pembayaran fidyah bagi orang yang belum melunasi hutang puasa tapi sudah masuk bulan Ramadhan lagi. Gunakan kaidah dan aturan fiqih madzhab Syafii. UX simple saja.

# Product Definition

## Overview

A static website dedicated to the calculation of **fidyah** (fasting debt compensation) based on the **Syafii School of Jurisprudence (Madzhab Syafii)**. The application specifically addresses cases where individuals have not yet settled their fasting debts before the arrival of the subsequent Ramadan.

## Target Audience

- **General Muslims:** Individuals needing to calculate their fidyah obligations.
- **Syafii Followers:** Those who adhere to the specific rulings of the Syafii school regarding fidyah doubling for multiple missed Ramadans.
- **Specialized Cases:** Specifically catering to women who missed fasts due to menstruation and failed to repay them before the next Ramadan.

## Key Features

- **Multi-year Calculation:** Support for calculating fidyah for fasts missed across several years, incorporating the Syafii rule where fidyah multiplies for each passing Ramadan.
- **Weight & Price Conversion:** Automatic conversion of "Mudd" units (approx. 675g-750g of staple food) into standard weights (e.g., kilograms of rice) or current monetary value.
- **Fiqh References:** Integrated explanations and dalil (legal evidence) from the Syafii school to educate users on the rulings behind their calculation.
- **Simple Calculation Logic:** A straightforward interface where users input the number of days missed and the number of years elapsed.

## User Experience (UX)

- **Single-page Minimalist:** A clean, focused interface that prioritizes speed and immediate results without unnecessary navigation.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both mobile and desktop users.
- **Respectful Aesthetic:** A calm and dignified UI reflecting the religious nature of the tool.

## Final Output

- **Total Calculation Summary:** A clear breakdown of the total amount of fidyah owed (in weight and estimated price).
- **Share/Download Result:** Functionality to save the calculation for personal records or share it via social platforms.
