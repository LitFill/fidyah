# Impeccable - Project Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Indonesian Muslims who have unpaid fasting debts (fidyah) from Ramadan. They need a clear, trustworthy tool to calculate their fidyah obligations according to Syafii Madzhab. Users range from everyday Muslims to those with specific religious knowledge, seeking reliable calculations for their spiritual obligations.

### Brand Personality
**Modern, Praktis, Cepat** (Modern, Practical, Fast)

The interface should feel contemporary yet respectful - blending efficiency with scholarly credibility. Not flashy or playful, but clean and trustworthy with a touch of Islamic educational heritage.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Clean, efficient, professional with Islamic scholarly undertones
- **Theme**: Dual-mode support - Light mode (default) and Dark mode
- **Color Direction**: 
  - Light: Stone/Emerald palette (current implementation works well)
  - Dark: Deep stone grays with emerald/green accents
- **Typography**: Serif for headings (scholarly feel), clear sans-serif readability for body
- **References**: Government e-services, modern Islamic apps (Islamicity, Muslim Pro)
- **Anti-references**: Cluttered layouts, cartoonish Islamic graphics, overly decorative patterns

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity First** - Every element must serve understanding. Numbers, calculations, and religious references should be immediately comprehensible.
2. **Trust Through Transparency** - Show the calculation formula. Display relevant Quran/hadith references. Make the methodology visible.
3. **Efficiency is Worship** - Fast, intuitive interactions respect the user's time and intention.
4. **Accessibility as Default** - Built-in large font and high contrast modes are essential, not afterthoughts.
5. **Scholarly But Approachable** - Use Islamic visual language subtly. Serif headings suggest authority; clean spacing suggests modern utility.
